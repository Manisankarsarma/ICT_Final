IRON-HIDE

SECURE ONLINE WEB VAULT FOR STORING YOUR CONFIDENTIAL DOCUMENTS

ENCRYPTION

Iron hide uses document
level client side encryption.
So that your data is
protected everywhere in
the cloud, both at rest and
in transit.

 FOR YOUR EYES ONLY

You own your data. Iron-
hide is designed as a zero
knowledge architecture.So
we don't store the encryption
keys. Hence, only you can
see your data.

 COLLABORATION

Iron hide has support
for enterprise teams.
Groups of users can have
shared access to a
set of confidential
files.

NO MORE VPN

You can access your
confidential documents
remotely, fully knowing
that they will be protected
during transit. So, no more
VPNs.

 ACCESS ANYWHERE

We have designed Iron Hide
as a completely browser based
web application. So, no plugins,
no desktop installations.
Access your data from any
Computer/Smartphone.
